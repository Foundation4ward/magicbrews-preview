How the magic works.

Three principles that make MagicBrews different from yet-another-AI-tool.

🧙

A familiar named Pepper

Pepper is the agent who tends the cauldron — your in-house AI familiar. She remembers your last week. She knows your funder Annette's email phrasing. She gets better the more brews you cast.

⚖️

The auto-close envelope

You decide what Pepper closes without asking — schema fixes, label hygiene, prospect appends. Every morning, the brews she finished overnight are listed, inspectable, reversible. The envelope widens only as your trust grows.

🛡️

Sovereignty wards

PII routes to on-device Ollama. The audit log is cryptographically signed. Delete is mathematical, not aspirational. Export a compliance bundle for HHS, GDPR, or your board.

Common questions

What does "tap a Brew" actually do?▾

When you tap "Plan date night," Pepper (the familiar inside the workshop) checks your partner's calendar, picks a restaurant from your past hits, makes the reservation, books a sitter, sends both confirmations — across 5 different services — and shows you the result. You see every step. You can interrupt anytime.

Do you store my emails?▾

No. We read through your existing provider via Model Context Protocol — your data stays in Gmail, Outlook, Proton wherever it already is. We cache only what's needed for the current view, encrypted, and forget it within the session.

What's the AI cost on top of the subscription?▾

Apprentice (free) and Adept tiers bring your own Anthropic key — you pay Anthropic directly for what Pepper uses. Sorcerer + Grandmaster include managed Anthropic + OpenAI inference within the daily cap ($60/day for Sorcerer, custom for Grandmaster). Above the cap, the cauldron degrades to Ollama (free, local) rather than auto-billing you.

Can I use this on my phone?▾

Yes — MagicBrews is mobile-first. The dashboard is a PWA so it installs to your home screen and works offline for cached views. Voice via Pepper works on iOS and Android. A native iOS app is in early access for Pro+.

How do you handle 20 different email accounts?▾

Each account connects via MCP (Gmail OAuth, Outlook OAuth, Proton Bridge, IMAP for everything else). The unified inbox shows them all in one stream with a provider-color corner badge so you instantly see which mailbox an email landed in. Filter chips switch the view to one provider at a time.

What about my privacy?▾

Sovereignty mode (included in Pro+) routes PII-sensitive prompts to on-device Ollama — they never leave your laptop. Audit log is cryptographically signed: any future regulator can verify what Pepper did. Right-to-delete rotates the signing key — the data is mathematically inaccessible, not "soft-deleted."

Who built this?▾

Master Yusuf Siraj — solo founder, Canadian nonprofit ED, built MagicBrews because he was running his own ops across 15+ surfaces and couldn't find a workshop shaped for solo strategic operators. The first cauldron tester is his own nonprofit (FFPF, $4.7M raised). Early access opening now.
